Air Pollution: The Silent Killer - What Causes It?

By Science Week Group 9A
Urban sprawl: Cities like Delhi pay the price for unchecked emissions.

Air pollution is the invisible enemy, caused by harmful substances from human activities. From exhaust fumes to industrial waste, it's turning our skies toxic.

Major Causes Uncovered

  • Vehicle Emissions: Cars and trucks release CO and NOx, fueling smog in traffic-heavy areas.
  • Industrial Smoke: Factories emit particulates, worsening air in zones like NCR's outskirts.
  • Fossil Fuels: Burning coal/oil traps heat and creates greenhouse gases.
  • Agriculture: Pesticides and stubble burning (peak Oct-Nov 2025) add ammonia and smoke.
  • Household Pollution: Wood/trash burning pollutes indoors and out.

Health Impacts and How to Fight Back

By Science Week Group 9A
Masks and caution: Essential gear in polluted cities like Delhi.

Pollution attacks our bodies—causing diseases and deaths. But solutions exist for a cleaner future.

Devastating Effects on Health

  • Respiratory Issues: Asthma, bronchitis, lung cancer from particles.
  • Heart Problems: Strokes and attacks from inflamed vessels.
  • Children's Risks: Brain damage and infections in kids.
  • Global Deaths: 7 million+ yearly (WHO).

Solutions for a Cleaner Tomorrow

  • Green Transport: Cycle, walk, or use public transit.
  • Renewables: Switch to solar/wind energy.
  • Plant Trees: Absorb CO2 in urban areas.
  • Reduce Waste: Recycle to avoid burning.
  • Policy Support: Back clean air laws.
